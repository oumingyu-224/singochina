A dinner suit is designed for ceremony and celebration. Its core purpose is to make a visual impact. This is why you see features like satin or sequin lapels and polished, formal accessories. What is Business Suit?

A business suit is a formal garment, usually consisting of a blazer, pants and a shirt, which is used in formal occasions to reflect professional image and professionalism. It is not only clothing, but also a symbol of professional image, carrying the individual’s professionalism and social status.

Main features:

  • Fabrics: Business suits are usually made of high-quality materials such as wool, cotton and blended fabrics, which not only ensure the comfort of wearing, but also reflect the texture of the garment.
  • Color: The color of business suits is dominated by dark colors, such as black, dark blue, gray, etc., which gives people a stable and professional impression.
  • Tailoring: Business suits usually adopt simple and generous design, tailored to fit, focusing on practicality and comfort.
  • Accessories: Business suits are often paired with shirts, ties, formal shoes, etc., and the overall match should focus on details and coordination.

When to Wear a Business Suit

Business suits are suitable for formal occasions, such as offices, meetings, interviews, business negotiations, important business activities. It is not only suitable for traditional formal occasions, but also for professional occasions in the modern workplace.

Professional occasions:

  • Office: business suit is an important part of office dress code, reflecting professional image and professionalism.
  • Meetings and Interviews: Business suits are an important part of interview and business meeting attire, enhancing personal image and professionalism.
  • Formal daytime activities: Business suits are suitable for formal daytime activities, such as business cocktail parties and formal occasions.

What is a Dinner Suit? What does “dinner suit” mean?

A dinner suit is a traditional Western semi-formal dress code, usually referring to the signature features of a man’s dress for formal occasions: a shiny satin lapel, a straight trouser line down the front, and a white wing collar and French cufflinks on the shirt, usually accompanied by a black or white bow tie.

Is a tuxedo a dinner suit?

A tuxedo, also called “black tie” and “formal evening wear” is often considered to be a type of dinner dress, and is especially worn for formal occasions such as dinners and weddings.

In British parlance, a tuxedo is often referred to as a “Dinner Jacket” or “Dinner Suit”, while in American parlance it is more commonly referred to as a “Tuxedo. “

Why is it called a dinner suit?

The tuxedo is called a “dinner suit” because it was originally designed for evening formal occasions, especially in 18th century England, with the rise of social activities, the tuxedo gradually became the symbol of evening formal occasions. 19th century, the tuxedo became popular among the British high society, and became the symbol of evening formal activities.

The tuxedo takes its name from the swallowtail design of its hem, which may have originally been associated with horseback riding, but later evolved into a feature of formal evening dress. The formal name of the tuxedo, “Evening Dress” or “Tuxedo”, was adopted globally in the 19th century and became internationally recognized as formal evening wear.

Can a Tuxedo Be Worn in the Office?

Styling Rules for Dinner Suits

Can you wear a normal tie with a dinner suit?

Wearing a normal tie with a dinner suit is usually not recommended. Tuxedos are already very formal in nature and are usually worn with a formal bow tie or collarless design to maintain a sense of coherence and formality. A plain tie may seem incongruous or even informal.

What shirt do you wear with a dinner suit?

Shirts worn with a dinner suit are usually formal in style, such as a wing collar, lapel, or pleated collar, which are more appropriate for formal occasions.

We do not recommend wearing a standard business shirt, as the design and cut is more oriented towards everyday or business occasions and lacks the formal touches and details required for a dinner suit, such as French cuffs, tie clips, etc., making it unsuitable for wearing with a dinner suit.

How to Style Your Dinner Suit? – Using accessories

  • Vest or belt: A vest or belt can add a sense of formality and layers to the overall look.
  • Pocket Scarf: Pocket scarf can add highlight and formality to the overall look. Not only is it a decorative piece, but it also expresses personality and style through color, pattern and folding.
  • Shoes (Patent Leather and Formal Oxfords): Patent leather shoes are usually more sophisticated, while formal oxfords are more solid, depending on the style of the dress and the occasion.
