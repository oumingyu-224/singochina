COMPANY INTRODUCTION

SINGO Holding Co., LTD. was founded in 1996 as a leading joint-stock enterprise specializing in clothing and investment sectors. Having been publicly listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange in 2007, our company boasts a network of over 1600 integrated online and offline sales outlets. With production bases in SHANDONG QINGDAO, Shanghai, and Hefei, we achieve an annual retail revenue exceeding 5 billion yuan, securing a position among the top 100 enterprises in China's garment industry.
