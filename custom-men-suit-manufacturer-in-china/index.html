Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

We are built for partnership. For our custom retail and boutique partners, our MOQ is just 1 set. For menswear brand orders, the typical volume is between 50-1000 sets per order.

What is your standard production time and delivery?

Our standard production lead time is 7 business days. We offer 7-day global delivery to over 90 countries. For bulk orders (500+ sets), the shipping time is 15 days.

Do you offer private label services?

Yes, this is a core specialty. We work with you to create a private label partnership, including custom branding, logo embroidery, and packaging to ensure the final product is 100% your brand.

What types of fabrics can I access through your fabric books?

We offer thousands of EU-standard fabrics. Our resources include both high-quality domestic and imported materials like Wool, Cotton, Silk, Linen, Merino Wool, and Worsted Wool.

How do you ensure quality control and consistency across orders?

Quality is ensured through our intelligent system. We use AI measurement for precision, a C2M system for consistency, modularized production to minimize errors, a 99.9% quality inspection rate, and make all orders fully traceable.
